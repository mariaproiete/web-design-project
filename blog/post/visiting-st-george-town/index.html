Visiting St. George’s town

Around the rock


Read this post in Portuguese: Como tudo começou

Tags: ["tourism", "tourists", "new comers", "photos", "around the rock", "st georges parish", "east side", "city hall", "island", "Bermuda", "photos"]

Publish date: 22 Jan, 2013 • by Maria Proiete

This couple of weeks have been very busy unpacking, house cleaning, doing errands and all that is need to settling down here and I was needing a break. Taking advantage of the fine weather – today was a break from all the rain of the last days – after lunch I went to visit the town of St. George, on the east side of the island. I went to the city (Hamilton) and I caught the #11 BUS to St. George… It was more or less a 45 minutes’ ride. As I thought the last BUS stop was in St. George’s Town I went until the last stop of the BUS route

First lesson learned: Get of 2 BUS stops before the end of the route ☺ … Gladly it was going down the hill and I enjoy the walk.

Here some of the photos I’ve took there.

Time flew by so fast that soon was 5pm and I should start heading home. Caught the BUS to Hamilton and when I got there it was already getting dark. The City Hall at nigh is also beautiful.


It’s after 6pm and I still have to take another BUS to get home, pass by the grocery shop and cook the dinner…
